Our Story

A five-year journey that began with a text exchange on the dating app Hinge and a first date at the Library Cafe.

After a year and a half of dating, Michelle and Christopher moved into a cozy apartment together in West Babylon. A year later they added to their family, adopting a chihuahua-rat terrier mix named Scarlet.

Over the course of their relationship, Michelle and Christopher have navigated major challenges, including a global pandemic, the loss of several loved ones, and the uncertain state of U.S. democracy. Through it all, their love for each other has remained steadfast.

Marking their five-year anniversary, Christopher finally got down on one knee to tie the knot officially.
